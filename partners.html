Partners

 

At Liessen Institute Academy, our commitment to providing exceptional educational opportunities knows no boundaries. We are proud to have established a robust network of prestigious universities and renowned corporate training partners across the globe. This extensive reach allows us to offer a truly global learning experience and ensures that our courses are accessible to individuals and organizations worldwide.
Our partnerships with leading universities enable us to bring you the expertise and academic rigor of esteemed institutions. From the Singapore University to the University of Monaco, alongside collaborations with esteemed departments such as Harvard Learning and Harvard Academy, we have curated an unparalleled selection of courses that span diverse subjects.
Additionally, our connections with corporate training partners extend our reach to the business world, providing specialized training programs tailored to meet the unique needs of organizations. We collaborate with companies from various industries, combining their industry insights with our educational expertise to deliver impactful and practical learning experiences.
